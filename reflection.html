When I got the chance to choose an elective, instead of going for something familiar and journalism-related, I decided to step out of my comfort zone and try something new. I should note that this report, while individually written, would not be possible without the collaboration of my teammates who played a part in interviewing three out of the four key publics individuals, and transcribed them for analysis in this report.

9. Research Poster example

I am a double major in Psychology/Journalism. In my PSY 3114 - Research Methods course, we learned how to conduct psychological studies from top to bottom. For our final project, we had to conceptualize and come up with our own research studies from scratch. Since Brooklyn Nine-Nine was my favorite TV show at the time, I decided to compare attitudes towards police officers before and after half the research subjects watched excerpts of the show, with the other half watching a clip from "The Good Place" as a control. The process from start to finish was one of the most enriching learning experiences, and I learned how to translate research methods learned in the classroom into an actual study.
