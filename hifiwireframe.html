Welcome to Singapore Food Paradise!

Nasi Lemak

Nasi lemak is a dish that comprises rice made fragrant with coconut cream and pandan leaves. It is believed to be Malay in origin, and traditionally accompanied by fried anchovies, sliced cucumbers, either fried chicken or fried fish, and a sweet chili sauce (Source: Wikipedia)

Laksa

Laksa consists of thick wheat noodles or rice vermicelli with chicken, prawn or fish, served in spicy soup based on either rich and spicy curry coconut milk or on sour asam (tamarind or gelugur). It can be found in different variations across Southeast Asia. (Source: Wikipedia)

Chicken Rice

The Hainanese chicken rice is a dish that consists of succulent steamed white chicken cut into bite-size pieces and served on fragrant rice with some light soy sauce. The dish is topped with sprigs of coriander leaf and sesame oil, and accompanied by a garlic-chilli dip.(Source: NLB Singapore)
